Ryan Luo's Biography

Ryan Luo is a Junior at Syracuse University studying Business Management at Whitman School of Management and Information Management and Technology in the iSchool. In Management, Ryan has a strong interest in Human Resources Management and Project Management.

Ryan has always been a hard working student who perseveres through difficult situations. In Spring of 2020, Ryan received his first academic honor at Syracuse University; being a Dean's list recipient for that semester and the following Fall 2020 semester In the summer of 2019, Ryan held an internship at Boston Medical Center as a Health Care Revenue Cycle analyst in the Finance department. At university, Ryan was an executive board member of the Syracuse University Esports Club and served as the Event Director of that club.
